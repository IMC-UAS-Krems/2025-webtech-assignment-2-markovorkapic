FamCharity

Help families recover from natural disasters!

Hello, we have started this charity because of the recent natural disasters that have ravaged the world and parts of space. We are here to provide aid to all families effected by natural disasters. Our goal is to make their lives easier and help them in these trying times while they recover. Donate now and help us help others with a small or large donation. Together we can make the world a better place.

You can help by volunteering at our local shelters where we house families after earthquakes and/or tsunamis destroy their homes. You can also donate old clothes food and other items directly to our address: Totally real Street 67, Tottally real town, 8900, Agartha.
